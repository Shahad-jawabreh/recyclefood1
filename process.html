The Food Recycle Process

We collect one of the world's most problematic wastes and turn it into one of the world's most sought after resources.

The Food Recycle process

1 Collection

2 Processing

3 Storage and analysis

4 Mixing

5 Packing and delivery

The Food Recycle process is a five part process from start to finish. We process each food waste stream separately, measure the nutritional content and then store them as ingredients to mix them together to make complete feeds.

Find out more

What is the food to feed ratio?

The Food Recycle process produces roughly a 50% output of animal feed from food waste, with the single by-product of evaporated water
